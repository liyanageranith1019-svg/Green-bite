Calories & Nutrition
Calories are units of energy that our bodies get from the foods and drinks we consume. They are essential for performing everyday activities like walking, working, and even breathing, as well as for more demanding tasks such as exercising. The amount of calories a person needs depends on their age, gender, weight, height, and activity level. Consuming too many calories can lead to weight gain and health problems, while too few can cause fatigue and weakness. Balancing calorie intake with energy expenditure helps maintain a healthy body weight, supports growth, and provides the energy needed for overall well-being.
