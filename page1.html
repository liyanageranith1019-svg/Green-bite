Nutrition Essentials
Good nutrition is the foundation of a healthy lifestyle. It involves consuming a balanced variety of foods that provide the nutrients your body needs to maintain health, feel good, and have energy. A well-balanced diet includes fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, and healthy fats while limiting processed foods, added sugars, and excessive sodium.
